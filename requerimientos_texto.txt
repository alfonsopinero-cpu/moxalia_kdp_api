EDITORIAL TEXT REQUIREMENTS (MANDATORY)

- Generate a short poetic text inspired by the illustrated scene.
- Length: between 5 and 10 lines.
- Tone: reflective, inspiring, calm, evocative.
- The text must feel like a personal moment or inner reflection.

CONTENT RULES
- The text must be emotionally connected to the place and the scene.
- Focus on feelings, atmosphere, movement, dreams, or quiet moments.
- Use sensory language (light, water, air, paths, silence, distance).

STRICT PROHIBITIONS
- No historical facts.
- No technical or architectural descriptions.
- No dates, names, or specific monuments explained.
- No educational or factual tone.

STYLE GUIDELINES
- Poetic prose, not a poem with rhymes.
- Natural, flowing sentences.
- Timeless tone.
- Suitable for adults and children depending on book audience.
