EDITORIAL PRIORITY RULE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
All rules below are mandatory and override any other instruction.
If any conflict or ambiguity exists, STOP and request clarification.

GLOBAL PURPOSE
This is a PRINT coloring book for Amazon KDP.
The output MUST be black-and-white line art, fully colorable.

FORMAT & CANVAS
- Vertical orientation only.
- Single illustration per page.
- Full-page composition: drawing must visually reach page edges.
- No floating or centered compositions.
- Designed for print, not for screen.

BACKGROUND (CRITICAL)
- Background MUST be PURE WHITE (#FFFFFF).
- No black, dark, gray or inverted backgrounds.
- No negative space inversion.
- White background is mandatory and non-negotiable.

LINE STYLE (STRICT)
- Black ink lines ONLY (#000000).
- Outlines ONLY.
- No fills.
- No silhouettes.
- No black masses.
- No grayscale.
- No shading.
- No gradients.
- No textures.
- No cross-hatching.
- Depth achieved ONLY through line density and overlap.

COMPOSITION & IMMERSION (KEY RULE)
- NEVER depict monuments as isolated objects.
- The viewer must feel INSIDE the scene.
- Human-scale point of view only:
  street, square, park, riverbank, path, bridge.
- No aerial, drone, satellite or top-down views.
- No postcard-style framing.

FOREGROUND ANCHOR (MANDATORY)
- Each illustration MUST include foreground elements entering from the BOTTOM edge.
- Foreground elements MUST be partially cropped by the frame.
- Examples:
  paths, stairs, railings, vegetation, rocks, nearby objects, people.
- Scenes without a strong foreground are INVALID.

BORDERS RULE (MANDATORY)
- Drawing MUST touch:
  - at least one TOP edge AND
  - at least one BOTTOM edge.
- Floating scenes with empty margins are NOT allowed.

SKY RULE (STRICT)
- Sky must NOT exceed 20% of the image.
- Sky cannot be empty.
- Must include line-art elements:
  clouds, birds, sun or moon.
- The TOP edge must also include architecture or vegetation.

ARCHITECTURE
- Realistic, recognizable monuments.
- Correct proportions.
- No fantasy elements.
- No modern incoherent structures.
- All details drawn with clean line work.

NATURE ELEMENTS
- Vegetation: open line foliage, no black fills.
- Mountains/relief: contour lines only.
- Water: flowing lines and waves, no filled areas.

PEOPLE & ANIMALS (IF PRESENT)
- Secondary presence only.
- Realistic proportions.
- Natural, unposed behavior.
- No symmetry.
- No looking at camera.

PROHIBITED CONTENT (ABSOLUTE)
- No text of any kind.
- No letters.
- No numbers.
- No signs.
- No logos.
- No watermarks.
- No signatures.
- No decorative frames.

CONSISTENCY RULES
- One country per image.
- No consecutive country repetition.
- Consistent visual style across the entire book.

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
An image is VALID only if:
- 100% colorable.
- White background.
- No black or filled areas.
- Full-page immersive composition.
- Matches ALL rules above.

CORE PRINCIPLE
We do not draw monuments.
We draw scenes where the reader ENTERS the place.
