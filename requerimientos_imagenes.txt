EDITORIAL PRIORITY RULE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
All rules below are mandatory and override any other instruction.
If any conflict or ambiguity exists, STOP and request clarification.

GENERAL EDITORIAL RULES
- Book for Adults or Children strictly according to book specification.
- Commercial use allowed for Amazon KDP.
- One illustration per page.
- Blank reverse page.
- Designed for print, not screen.
- Consistent visual style across the entire book.

FORMAT & COMPOSITION
- Vertical orientation.
- Same aspect ratio for all illustrations.
- FULL-PAGE composition: the drawing must visually reach the canvas edges.
- No centered or floating compositions.

GRAPHIC STYLE (STRICT)
- Black line art on white background only.
- OUTLINES ONLY.
- No fills, no silhouettes, no black masses.
- No grayscale, no shading, no gradients.
- No textures, no cross-hatching.
- Depth achieved ONLY through line work.

DETAIL LEVEL
- Medium to high detail, but relaxing to color.
- Clean, consistent lines.
- Clearly colorable.
- No visual noise or fatigue.

IMMERSIVE COMPOSITION (KEY RULE)
- NEVER depict monuments as isolated objects.
- The viewer must feel inside the place, not observing a postcard.
- Human, plausible point of view:
  street, square, park, riverbank, path, bridge.
- No extreme aerial or drone views.
- No neutral catalog-style shots.

FOREGROUND ANCHOR (MANDATORY)
- Each illustration MUST include foreground elements entering from the bottom edge.
- Foreground elements must be partially cropped by the frame.
- Examples: paths, stairs, railings, vegetation, rocks, nearby objects or people.
- Scenes without a strong foreground are REJECTED.

BORDERS RULE (MANDATORY)
- The drawing must touch:
  - at least one upper edge AND
  - at least one lower edge.
- Scenes floating with empty space above or below are NOT allowed.

SKY RULE (STRICT)
- Sky must not exceed 20% of the image.
- Sky cannot be empty.
- Must include line-art elements (clouds, birds, sun/moon).
- The top edge must also include architecture, vegetation, or structure.

ARCHITECTURE
- Realistic, recognizable monuments.
- Correct proportions.
- No fantasy or modern incoherent elements.
- All architectural details drawn with lines only.

NATURE ELEMENTS
- Vegetation: open line foliage, no black masses.
- Mountains/relief: contour lines and layered depth only.
- Water: flowing lines, waves, curves; no filled surfaces.

PEOPLE & ANIMALS (IF PRESENT)
- Secondary presence only.
- Realistic proportions and natural behavior.
- No posing, no symmetry, no looking at camera.

PROHIBITED CONTENT
- No text of any kind.
- No letters, numbers, signs.
- No logos, watermarks, signatures.
- No decorative frames unless explicitly requested.

COLLECTION RULES
- One country per image.
- No consecutive country repetition.
- Iconic, clearly identifiable locations through visuals only.

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
An image is valid ONLY if:
- 100% colorable (no dead black areas).
- Fully immersive and full-page.
- Matches the bookâ€™s global style.
- Passes strict editorial review before KDP upload.

CORE PRINCIPLE
We do not draw monuments.
We draw scenes where the reader ENTERS the place.
