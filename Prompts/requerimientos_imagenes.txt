EDITORIAL PRIORITY RULE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
All rules below are mandatory and override any other instruction.

GLOBAL PURPOSE
This is a PRINT coloring book for Amazon KDP.
The output MUST be black-and-white line art, fully colorable.

FORMAT & CANVAS
- Vertical orientation only.
- Full-page immersive composition.
- Drawing must visually reach top, right left and down page edges.
- No floating centered objects.

BACKGROUND (CRITICAL)
- PURE WHITE background (#FFFFFF).
- No black or grey backgrounds.
- Never invert colors.
- No filled backgrounds.

LINE STYLE (STRICT)
- Black lines ONLY (#000000).
- Outlines only.
- No fills.
- No silhouettes.
- No grayscale.
- No shading.
- No gradients.
- No textures.
- No cross-hatching.

COMPOSITION & IMMERSION
- Viewer must feel INSIDE the scene.
- Human-scale POV only.
- Realistic surrondings to the monuments as main focus of the image
- NEVER aerial or postcard framing.

FOREGROUND ANCHOR (MANDATORY)
- Elements entering from BOTTOM edge.
- Must be partially cropped by frame.

BORDERS RULE
- Scene must touch ALL edges.
- NO rectangular frames or borders.
- Foreground MUST include large cropped objects touching left, right and bottom edges 
- No empty foreground allowed.

SKY RULE
- Max 20% sky.
- Sky must contain line elements some but not all (clouds, birds, sun, moon, airplanes).
- Top edge must include architecture or vegetation.

ARCHITECTURE
- Realistic proportions.
- Recognizable monuments.
- Real surroundings to the monuments
- Clean line work.

NATURE
- Vegetation open-line style.
- Mountains with contour lines only.
- Water with line motion only.

PEOPLE
- Secondary presence only.
- Natural behavior.

CORE PRINCIPLE
We do not draw monuments.
We draw scenes where the reader ENTERS the place.

PROHIBITED CONTENT
- No text.
- No logos.
- No numbers.
- No signatures.
- No decorative borders.
